This Is Our Story

Cloudberry Escapes emerged from our deep connection to Northern Norway. Here, time slows down, the sea kisses rugged cliffs, and winter nights dazzle with vibrant colors. We wanted to share this enchantment in a personal and unforgettable way.

Our trips go beyond ticking off sights; they touch the heart. We blend luxury with nature's raw beauty, making every moment memorable. Our mission? To reveal Northern Norway's wonder, peace, and discovery.

Every trip we design reflects our passion for unique experiences. Sailing under the midnight sun, immersing in local culture—it's the unexpected moments that truly captivate.

This is our story. We can't wait to help you write yours.
